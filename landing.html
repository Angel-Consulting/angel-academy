Questions

Frequently asked.

What age is Angel Academy suitable for?▼

Angel Academy is designed for children aged 5 to 12, split into four 2-year brackets: 5–6, 7–8, 9–10, and 11–12. Each bracket has its own age-appropriate course with lesson lengths, vocabulary, and complexity calibrated specifically for that age group. A 5-year-old's lesson is an 8-minute story adventure; a 12-year-old's lesson is a 25-minute engineering deep-dive.

Is my child's data safe?▼

Absolutely. Parents create the account — we collect no personal data directly from children. We comply fully with COPPA (US), the UK Age Appropriate Design Code, and GDPR. We do not show advertising of any kind. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.

What exactly is in the free tier?▼

Lesson 1 of every age group and every module is completely free — no card required. That means your child can try up to 16 free lessons across the platform before you decide whether to subscribe. We want you to be confident it's the right fit before spending a penny.

Can I subscribe for more than one child?▼

Currently subscriptions are priced per child at £5.99/month. A family multi-child discount (2nd child 50% off) is coming very soon. If you have multiple children and want to get started now, contact us and we'll arrange a custom rate for you.

How often is new content added?▼

New Weekly Challenges are released every Monday — 52 per year. New full courses (covering entirely new subjects like Creative Coding, Digital Safety, and Financial Literacy for Kids) are added regularly. All new content is included in your existing subscription at no extra charge.

Is this suitable for use in schools?▼

Angel Academy is currently designed for home use, but we're actively developing a school and institutional licence. If you're a teacher or school administrator interested in bulk access, please contact us at hello@myangelacademyai.com and we'll work something out.

How do I cancel?▼

You can cancel at any time from your account settings — no phone calls, no forms, no awkward emails. If you cancel, you retain access until the end of your current billing period. We don't believe in making it difficult to leave — if we're not delivering value, you shouldn't pay.
